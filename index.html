Moon trying to stop Cassie from defeating him and freeing Sun. Moon, also known as Moondrop, is a major antagonist in Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach and a minor antagonist in both its sequel DLC, Ruin, and the Tales from the Pizzaplex anthology novel series. He will return in the upcoming Five Nights at Freddy's: Help Wanted 2.
